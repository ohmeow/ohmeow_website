About Me

Wayde Gilliam (ohmeow)
I’m a full stack web application and AI/ML developer with well over 20 years in the business.

In addition to being the author of blurr, a python library that enables fast.ai developers to train Hugging Face transformers for core NLP tasks, I’m also a proud fast.ai community leader, where I’m active in both contributing to their deep learning library and helping folks out on the forums and discord.

Outside of work, I enjoy hanging out with my two labradors, reading, working out, and above all else, learning. Feel free to give me a follow on twitter or github if any of the above interests you.

Feel free to reach out to me via email or direct message on X.
